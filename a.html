Philippine Navy Officer Candidate School

The Philippine Navy Officer Candidate School, strategically located at the heart of Zambales, is a Naval school producing competent graduates in the naval aspect, conforming to the standards of the Philippine Navy. It is being directed by NOQC and NOCC graduates, as well as graduates from the Philippine Military Academy. To date, the Philippine Navy Officer Candidate School, or PNOCS, already had produced twenty-five strong and credible classes, composed of not more than 50 students each class.

MISSION

Provide pre-entry training to covilians and probationary officers in order to equip them with the knowledge, skills, and attitude required for a Naval Officer.
