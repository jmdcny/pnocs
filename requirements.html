General Requirements

  • Must be a natural-born Filipino Citizen,
  • At least 5 feet in height,
  • Must be of good moral character with no pending civil or criminal case,
  • Single, never been married, with no child or children to support,
  • Must be physically and mentally fit,
  • Must be 21 - 28 years old,
  • Must be a graduate of any technical course, and
  • Must pass the AFP Aptitude Battery Test, IQ and Special Written Examination.

Documentary Requirements

  • Original and Photocopy of Birth Certificate
  • Original and Photocopy of Transcript of Records
  • Original and Photocopy of College Diploma
  • 2"x2" colored picture (2 copies)
  • Accomplished Application Form
  • NBI Clearance
  • Drug Test Result
