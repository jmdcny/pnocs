NOCC Timeline

     The NOCC "Mandarangan" Class 26 conducted a window tour at the Naval Combat Engineering Brigade, and the Philippine Marine Corps at the Bonifacio Naval Station, Taguig City on 29 July 2015. The class, as accompanied by the PNOCS Assistant Commandant and their course director, were welcomed by NOCC alumni assigned to departments in the offices visited. Briefings were conducted by most competent authority or personnel to give the students the ideas about the units, and perhaps to invite volunteers from the class to be part of their unit, expecially to the Philippine Marine Corps.

     Forty-two (42) members, originally 50, of NOCC "SINAGTALA" Class 27 are bound to be recognized as part of the NETC Student Corps come 13 and 14 og August 2015, Pre-recognition and Recognition Rites, respectively. The recognition rites marks the end of dumbgardship and the beginning of a new chapter in the life of an Officer-Candidates.
