Naval Officer Candidate Course: The Honor Code

"We, the Officer-Candidates, Do Not Lie, Cheat, Steal, nor Tolerate Among Us those who do."


2 Basic Questions:

1. Do I intend to deceive?

2. Do I intend to take undue advantage?

If I answer 'NO' to both questions, then I'm doing the Honorable thing.
